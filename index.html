      How to Play

      • Swap adjacent tiles to match 3 or more identical emojis.
      • 🔴🔴🔴 Matching 3 clears those tiles.
      • 🟢🟢🟢🟢 Matching 4 clears a row or column.
      • 🟣🟣🟣🟣🟣 Matching 5 clears the entire board.
      • Earn a Shuffle every 100 points.
      • The board grows larger every 5 levels.
